// questions

Things you might be wondering.

The real answers — price, timeline, and what it's actually like to work with me.

How much does an engagement cost?

Most projects are fixed-price between $1,500 and $8,000 depending on scope and integrations. I share a flat quote within 48 hours of our scoping call — no hourly surprises, no scope creep.

How fast can you actually ship?

A typical automation (lead outreach, AI agent, or CRM sync) ships live in 2–4 weeks. Larger multi-system builds run 4–8 weeks. I don't take a project if I can't commit to a realistic end date.

What tools do you build on?

n8n is my workhorse for workflow automation. Claude and Gemini for agentic AI. Apollo, Google Workspace, Notion, Airtable, and HubSpot for CRM and data. I adapt to your stack rather than the reverse.

Do you work with non-technical founders?

Most of my clients are non-technical. I translate business problems into automations and hand over Loom walkthroughs your team can follow. You don't need to know anything about APIs.

What happens after the project ships?

30 days of post-launch support are included — bug fixes, tweaks, and questions over email or Loom. After that, I offer a monthly retainer for teams who want ongoing iteration, or I step away cleanly.

Do you take equity or revenue-share deals?

Occasionally — for early-stage African startups with a clear automation-as-moat thesis. Default is fixed-price. Let's talk on the call and see what makes sense.
